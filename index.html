Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 8, 2025

This app, Parcel Rate Estimator, does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users.

Data Collection

This app works primarily offline. However, for estimating parcel charges, it may make a network request to fetch the distance between stations. This is done solely to calculate parcel rates and does not involve collecting, storing, or sharing any personal information such as names, locations, contacts, or device identifiers.

Use of Public Data

The Parcel Rate Estimator app uses parcel fare data derived from publicly available Indian Railways documents and tariff tables. This information is sourced directly from Indian Railways' official parcel rate portal: https://parcel.indianrail.gov.in/parcel_rates.jsp . All data is accessed solely for informational purposes to assist users.

Third-party Access

This app does not use third-party analytics, advertising services, or any backend servers. No user information is sent to external systems.

Disclaimer

This app is not affiliated with Indian Railways or any government entity. It is a privately developed tool meant to offer users a rough estimate of parcel charges. The rates shown are approximate and may differ due to periodic revisions or additional charges. Users are strongly advised to confirm final rates at official railway parcel booking counters.

Contact

If you have any questions, please contact the developer through the support section on the Google Play Store.
