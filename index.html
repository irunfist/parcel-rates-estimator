Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 8, 2025

This app, Parcel Rate Estimator, does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users.

Data Collection

This app operates fully offline and does not access, track, or collect any personal information such as names, locations, contacts, or device identifiers.

Use of Public Data

The app provides estimates based on publicly available Indian Railways data. It is meant for informational use only and is not affiliated with Indian Railways or any government entity.

Third-party Access

This app does not use third-party analytics, advertising services, or any backend servers.

Disclaimer

The rates shown in the app are approximate. Users are advised to verify actual rates at railway booking counters.

If you have any questions, you can contact the developer directly via the support section on the Play Store.
